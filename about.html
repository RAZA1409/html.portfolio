About Me

Mohammed Raza is a dedicated computer science student with a passion for technology and environmental advocacy. His academic pursuits are shaped by a strong desire to use his technical skills for the greater good. With a focus on sustainability, he is currently working on projects that address critical global challenges, such as desertification and endangered species conservation. His project, Endangered Earth, highlights the need to protect vulnerable species, while another project explores the threat desertification poses to arable lands.

Outside of academics, Raza is driven by a sense of responsibility to leverage his knowledge for positive environmental impact. He actively seeks to create innovative solutions that merge technology with sustainability. Through his work, he aims to raise awareness about pressing ecological issues and contribute to long-term, data-driven solutions that benefit both the planet and society.
